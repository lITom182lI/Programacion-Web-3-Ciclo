## 2. Prompt de Producción Maestro (Copia-Listo en Inglés Técnico)

Someter el siguiente bloque de prompt directamente en el motor **Gemini / Veo** para generar el video representativo:

```text
A seamless 60-second cinematic looped technological presentation for "Apex Nova Studio", structured in four chronological scenes:

[SCENE 1: IDENTITY & CONCEPT - 00:00 to 00:15]
A minimalist abstract 3D glassmorphism isotipo representation for "Apex Nova Studio" floating in the center of the screen. The background is a solid deep dark void #0b0f19. The isotipo glows softly with electric blue #3b82f6 and vibrant neon purple #8b5cf6 hues. Camera movement: very slow, continuous push-in zoom. Photorealistic rendering, ultra-high resolution, cinematic studio quality, 4K, 60fps.

[SCENE 2: ARCHITECTURE & DATA FLOW - 00:15 to 00:30]
The isotipo seamlessly expands into an isometric 3D system architecture layout, made of interconnected translucent glass blocks and floating nodes showing data streams. Glowing lines run between the blocks in electric blue #3b82f6, vibrant neon purple #8b5cf6, and emerald green #10b981 accents. The background remains deep dark #0b0f19. Camera movement: slow horizontal pan from left to right. Ultra-high resolution, cinematic studio quality, 4K, 60fps.

[SCENE 3: INTERFACE & RESPONSIVE UX - 00:30 to 00:45]
The blocks morph elegantly into sleek device frames (a laptop, a tablet, and a smartphone) displaying abstract responsive user interfaces. UI elements have modern Outfit typography and glassmorphic panels that transition smoothly between the screens. Luminous accents in electric blue #3b82f6 and neon purple #8b5cf6 glow against the deep dark background #0b0f19. Camera movement: slow, isometric 360-degree rotation. Ultra-high resolution, cinematic studio quality, 4K, 60fps.

[SCENE 4: CONCLUSION & LOOP CONVERGENCE - 00:45 to 01:00]
The devices dissolve into soft digital particles that converge back into the original minimalist abstract 3D glassmorphism isotipo from Scene 1. Luminous technical badges indicating "W3C HTML5" and "WCAG 2.2 Accessible" glow subtly at the bottom of the frame in emerald green #10b981. Camera movement: very slow pull-out zoom. The final frame at 01:00 matches the opening frame at 00:00 exactly to create a seamless infinite loop in the browser. Ultra-high resolution, cinematic studio quality, 4K, 60fps.
```

## 3. Resumen y Planificación del Storyboard (Español)

1. **Escena 1 (Concepto)**: Presentación de la marca mediante un isotipo abstracto en 3D glassmorphism y luces de neón en azul y púrpura sobre fondo oscuro. La cámara avanza lentamente hacia el objeto.
2. **Escena 2 (Arquitectura)**: El objeto se despliega como bloques de código y flujos de datos interconectados con acentos de color verde esmeralda. Movimiento de paneo horizontal.
3. **Escena 3 (Interfaz)**: Los bloques se integran en pantallas responsivas (laptops, celulares) reflejando un desarrollo adaptable e intuitivo. Rotación lenta en perspectiva isométrica.
4. **Escena 4 (Bucle)**: Las pantallas se disuelven de vuelta al isotipo original en 3D con sellos sutiles de accesibilidad WCAG y validación W3C. La cámara retrocede para encuadrar exactamente el frame inicial de la Escena 1, completando el ciclo sin saltos visuales.
